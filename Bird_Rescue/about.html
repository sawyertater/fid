About Feathered Friends Rescue

Founded in 1998, Feathered Friends Rescue has grown into a sanctuary and care center for birds of all types. Our dedicated team of bird specialists and volunteers work tirelessly to provide every bird a second chance.

Our Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate, and set free injured birds while educating the community on conservation.
