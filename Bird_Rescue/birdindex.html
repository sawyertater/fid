Welcome to Feathered Friends Rescue

Helping Birds Find Their Wings Again!

Feathered Friends Rescue is dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation, and release of injured and orphaned birds. We also provide permanent sanctuary for those that cannot be returned to the wild.

Our Services Include:

  • Emergency rescue
  • Veterinary care
  • Flight rehabilitation
  • Educational outreach

Check out our partners at Audubon Society for more birdy resources.
